Nadia Rayward

PROBLEM SOLVER - WORLD TRAVELER - FAST LEARNER

I am a UX/UI designer born and raised in Madrid, Spain to an American mother and a Palestinian father. I am a world traveler with a background in biology, photography and education.

I have done volunteer work in places such as South Africa, China, Vietnam, Greece, France and Belize and have visited over 30 countries including Ecuador, Cuba, Italy, Austria, Jordan, the United Arab Emirates and the Seychelles islands. I’m so grateful to have traveled so much as it has given me the opportunity to learn about different cultures and that has allowed me to connect with all sorts of people and understand their needs .

I’ve always been drawn to solving problems and after studying Biology in college, I worked as an IVF (In Vitro Fertilisation) lab technician where I realised I wasn’t meant to be sitting in dim light looking through a microscope and following very strict research protocols . I needed more creativity, more light and more adventure. I decided to do a MS in Biological Photography and Imaging to learn how to best support scientific research with photography and video while learning different techniques. During the Masters, I learned design tools such as Illustrator, InDesign and Photoshop and fell in love with digital design but didn’t really know what to do with this new interest.

Education has always been a field that has interested me and after the Masters I spent some months working as an ESL teacher in Spain . One of my biggest frustrations during this time was being able to teach a small group of people or a person at a time. I wanted to educate many people at the same time . During this period of time I found out about UX design and realised it was the perfect fit for me where I could research what people need and solve their problems creatively with a greater impact .

Having experienced so many fields in such a short period of time has given me the ability to learn fast . This is the reason why I love the tech industry; it is fast-paced, and there is always something new to learn.

UX is the combination of all my passions : problem-solving, research, design, creativity and learning about people. Coming from a multicultural and diverse background , and having had so many amazing experiences throughout my life , gives me the opportunity and ability to think about problem-solving with a unique perspective .
